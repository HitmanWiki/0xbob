FAQ

Common
Questions

How do we start a project? +

Just DM me on Telegram with a description of what you need. I'll ask a few questions to understand the scope, then send you a breakdown of deliverables, timeline, and cost within 24 hours. Most projects kick off within 48 hours of that first message.

How much does a project cost? +

Every project is scoped individually — there's no one-size-fits-all pricing in Web3 development. A simple ERC-20 contract is very different from a full DeFi protocol. I give clear, fixed-price quotes upfront so there are no surprises. Message me with your project and I'll give you a number.

Do you do audits? +

I write secure contracts with test coverage and internal review, but I'm not a formal security auditing firm. For high-value protocols I strongly recommend a professional audit from firms like Code4rena, Certik, or Sherlock in addition to my work. I'm happy to assist in preparing your code for audit.

What do I receive on project completion? +

You get the full source code, deployment scripts, technical documentation, verified contracts on block explorers, and a walkthrough call or video. Nothing is black-box. You own everything and can maintain it yourself or with any other developer after handoff.

Do you offer post-launch support? +

Yes. All projects include a support window (typically 7–30 days depending on scope) for bug fixes and questions. Beyond that, I offer ongoing retainer engagements for projects that need continuous development, upgrades, or monitoring.

Can you work on an existing codebase? +

Absolutely. I can review, extend, debug, and optimize existing smart contracts and DApps. Share the repo on Telegram and I'll take a look and tell you what's involved before any commitment.

Which chains do you support? +

All major EVM chains (Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, BSC, Avalanche, Blast, zkSync, and more), Solana, and TON. If your chain is EVM-compatible, I can deploy there. For non-EVM chains, ask and I'll confirm support.

How do payments work? +

Typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery for most projects. I accept crypto (ETH, USDC, USDT) or fiat via bank transfer. For ongoing retainers, monthly invoices. We'll agree on payment terms before starting — no surprises.
